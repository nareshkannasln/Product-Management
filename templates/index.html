Welcome to the Inventory Management System

This system helps you track products, locations, and product movements between locations.

Features

  • 📦 Manage Products – Add, update, and list your inventory items.
  • 📍 Manage Locations – Define storage locations like warehouses or stores.
  • 🔁 Track Movements – Monitor product transfers between locations.
  • 📊 View Reports – Generate summary reports for quick insights.

Navigation

Use the navigation bar above to access different sections. You can:

  • Click Products to add/edit product items.
  • Click Locations to manage warehouses or storage areas.
  • Click Movements to record item transfers.
  • Click Report to see current stock levels per location.
